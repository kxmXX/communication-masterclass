## Part 4: Platform-Specific Recommendations

### LinkedIn
**Do**: MicroвҶ’Macro, Insider's Confession, Authority Flare, Ultimate Question
**Don't**: Start with 'We', pure Data dump, ask for likes/shares
**Optimal length**: 200-400 words
**Best days**: Tuesday-Thursday 8-10am ET
**CTA**: Always a question, never "like and share"

### Crowdfunding (Kickstarter/Ulule)
**Do**: Underdog Story + Extreme Anchoring + Video + Social Proof HIGH
**Don't**: Vague timelines, renderings without prototype, generic testimonials
**Video length**: 60-90 seconds
**Reward tiers**: 4-5 tiers, $1 вҶ’ $25 вҶ’ $99 вҶ’ $299 вҶ’ $999
**Social proof**: MUST be above the fold

### Pitch Deck
**Slide 1**: Solution in One Phrase (5-8 words)
**Slide 2**: Problem вҶ’ Agitate вҶ’ Before/After
**Slide 3**: Why Now (3 reasons)
**Don't**: "Uber for X", too many slides, jargon
**Total slides**: 10-12 max

### Fundraising Email
**Subject**: Personal + curiosity gap
**Opening**: Reciprocity (mention their work)
**The ask**: Direct, early
**Why Now**: 2-3 lines
**CTA**: Low pressure ("15 min call?")

### Conference Keynote
**Structure**: Three-Act (hero's journey)
**Must include**: Live demo, personal story, big reveal
**Emotional arc**: Setup вҶ’ Challenge вҶ’ Triumph вҶ’ Call to action
**Don't**: Read slides, corporate speak, more than 20 minutes without demo
